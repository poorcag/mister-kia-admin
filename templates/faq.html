Who is Mr. Know-it-all?

He's your friendly AI teacher! Here to answer all of your questions in a polite and friendly way.

How does Mr. Know-it-all work?

We use OpenAI's whisper model to convert your voice to text, which is then used to prompt ChatGPT for an answer. ElevenLabs is used to perform text-to-speech conversion to provide a friendly audible response.

How accurate/safe are the responses?

Mr. Know-it-all has all the information of the GPT-3.5 Turbo enterprise model which has been fine tuned for dialog. You can read more about OpenAI's safety standards here

Are my questions private?

We don't collect, store, share, or sell data on the content of any questions asked. I believe user privacy is paramount.

Is Mr. Know-it-all free?
For the time being, as the service is still being built and tested. I will eventually implement a paid token system to support the site and allow it to remain operational.
Why did you make Mr. Know-it-all?

My kids loved asking questions to Google Assistant but I found the answers were always poor quality. I made this primarily for my kids to enjoy but have decided to open it up to more users.
